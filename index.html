Our Story

Eighteen years of beautiful craft

Founded in 2007 in the heart of Dhaka, Asha Furniture began as a small workshop with one simple belief: that every Bangladeshi home deserves furniture built to last generations.

Today, we employ over 200 skilled artisans across three workshops, serving customers in every major city.

⛭

Our journey

2007

Asha Furniture is founded in Bashundhara with a 4-person team and a passion for solid wood joinery.

2011

We open our first showroom in Gulshan, Dhaka — 5,000 sqft of curated living spaces.

2015

Expansion to Chittagong and Sylhet. Launch of our custom furniture service.

2019

10,000 homes furnished. We introduce eco-certified materials and sustainable packaging.

2026

Launching our online store to bring premium craftsmanship to every corner of Bangladesh.

Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free delivery? +
Yes! We offer free delivery within Dhaka for all orders above ৳10,000. For other cities including Chittagong, Sylhet and Rajshahi, delivery is free for orders above ৳25,000.
How long does delivery take? +
Within Dhaka: 2–4 business days. Chittagong & Sylhet: 5–7 business days. Other cities: 7–10 business days. Custom orders may take 3–6 weeks.
Do you offer assembly service? +
Yes, free in-home assembly is included with every order within Dhaka. For other cities, assembly is available at a nominal fee.
What is your return policy? +
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the item is in original condition and there is a manufacturing defect. Custom orders are non-returnable.
Do you take custom orders? +
Absolutely! Custom furniture is one of our specialities. Contact us with your requirements and we'll provide a quote within 24 hours.
What warranty do you offer? +
All solid wood structural components carry a 10-year warranty. Upholstery and fabric carry a 2-year warranty. Electronics carry a 1-year warranty.
Can I pay in instalments (EMI)? +
Yes! We offer 0% EMI plans through Dutch-Bangla Bank, BRAC Bank and City Bank credit cards for orders above ৳20,000.
What wood types do you use? +
We use solid teak, American walnut, sheesham (rosewood), mahogany and oak — all FSC-certified and sourced from sustainable forests.
